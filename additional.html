Additional Services

In addition to our core immigration services, we offer assistance with travel documents or paroles, our experienced team is here to guide you through the process.

Advance Parole

Advance Parole is a travel document issued by U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) that allows certain non-citizens to re-enter the U.S. without abandoning their pending immigration status. It is commonly used by individuals with pending green card applications, DACA recipients, and those with Temporary Protected Status.

B1/B2 Visa

The B-1/B-2 visa is a non-immigrant visa for individuals traveling to the U.S. temporarily for business, tourism, or medical treatment.

Parole in Place (PIP)

PIP is a special immigration benefit that allows certain undocumented family members of U.S. military personnel and veterans to remain in the U.S. without fear of deportation and apply for a green card without having to leave the country.
