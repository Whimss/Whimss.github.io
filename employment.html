Employment

Temporary Work Visas

Our legal team specializes in managing a range of temporary employment visas. We work with you to assess your qualifications and job role, ensuring your application is submitted under the most appropriate category. We offer comprehensive support throughout the entire application process, ensuring a smooth and efficient experience from start to finish.

E-1

Treaty Traders – Allows individuals from treaty countries to enter the U.S. for substantial trade between their home country and the U.S.

E-2

Treaty Investors - Grants entry to investors from treaty countries who make a significant investment in a U.S. business

E-3

Specialty Occupation Workers from Australia - This is a U.S. work visa specifically for Australian citizens who seek to work in a specialty occupation in the United States. It is similar to the H-1B visa but exclusively available to Australians.

H-1B

Specialty Occupations - For skilled workers in specialty fields requiring a bachelor’s degree or higher, such as IT, engineering, and finance.

L-1

Intracompany Transferee Executive or Manager - This visa allows companies to transfer employees from a foreign office to a U.S. office

O-1

Extraordinary Ability in Science, Art, Education, Business, or Athletics - Granted to individuals with extraordinary ability or achievement in their field.

TN Visa

Professionals from Canada and Mexico - Allows qualified Canadian and Mexican professionals to work in the U.S. under NAFTA/USMCA agreements.

Permanent Residency through Employment

Our legal team specializes in handling employment-based green card applications under the EB-1, EB-2, and EB-3 categories. We work closely with you to assess your qualifications and job role, ensuring you apply under the most appropriate category. From start to finish, we provide comprehensive guidance to streamline the application process.

EB-1

This visa is reserved for individuals with extraordinary abilities, outstanding professors or researchers, and multinational executives or managers.

EB-2

This visa is designed for professionals with advanced degrees or individuals with exceptional ability in their field

EB-3

This visa is designated for skilled workers, professionals, and other workers with at least two years of experience
